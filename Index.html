JEAN-PHILIPPE COUTURE

Lawyer, Legal Innovation Enthusiast and Entrepreneur

Jean-Philippe is an experienced tax and corporate lawyer regularly advising clients on a broad range of corporate and tax law issues, with a focus on matters relating to family office and private business corporate reorganization and transactions.

After practicing law for several years as a partner with both one of Canada's largest law firm and a 'big 4' professional firm, Jean-Philippe founded Ingenio Solutions Ltd., a Calgary-based legal technology firm developing and implementing innovative for the corporate law practice.
